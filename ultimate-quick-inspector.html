Ultimate Quick Inspector

Pin, organize, and instantly access your most-used assets and scene objects in Unity.

Supercharge Your Workflow

Ultimate Quick Inspector is a powerful yet lightweight editor extension that boosts your productivity by providing one-click access to everything that matters—whether it's scene objects, prefabs, materials, or scripts.

Intuitive & Powerful Features

Pin Anything

Instantly access your favorite prefabs, scripts, materials, or GameObjects without needing to search the Project or Hierarchy windows.

Multi-Page Organization

Create unlimited custom pages, such as “UI Elements,” “Player Systems,” or “Level Tools,” to stay perfectly organized.

Drag-and-Drop Simplicity

Add items with a simple drag-and-drop motion—no setup required. It's the fastest way to build your personal toolbox.

Robust Scene Object Tracking

Maintains its connection to GameObjects using a reliable GUID system, even if you rename or disable them.
