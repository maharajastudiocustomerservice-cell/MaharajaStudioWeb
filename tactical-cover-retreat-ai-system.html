Advanced Tactical Features

High-Performance Async Core

Utilizes Unity's Jobs System and Burst Compiler to perform thousands of calculations on worker threads, ensuring smooth performance with zero frame drops.

Advanced Tactical Scoring

A modular, weight-based engine with 20+ unique scorers to define AI personality, including path safety, ambush potential, and crossfire analysis.

Designer-Friendly Workflow

Empower designers to guide AI behavior with intuitive tools like Tactical Zones, Cover Nodes, and Hazard volumes, no coding required.

Modular & Extensible

The entire system is driven by ScriptableObjects. Easily create your own Providers and Scorers to implement unique AI behaviors specific to your game.
