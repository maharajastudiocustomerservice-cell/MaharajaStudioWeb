✅ Ultimate Quick Inspector – Boost Your Unity Workflow!
Pin, organize, and instantly access your most-used assets and scene objects in Unity!
Ultimate Quick Inspector is a powerful yet lightweight editor extension that supercharges your productivity by providing one-click access to everything that matters—whether it's scene objects, prefabs, materials, or scripts.
💡 Key Features
📌 Pin Anything: Instantly access your favorite prefabs, scripts, materials, or GameObjects without needing to search the Project or Hierarchy windows.
📁 Multi-Page Organization: Create unlimited custom pages, such as “UI Elements,” “Player Systems,” or “Level Tools,” to stay perfectly organized.
➡️ Drag-and-Drop Simplicity: Add items with a simple drag-and-drop motion—no setup required.
🔍 Smart Search & Filter: Quickly locate items with the built-in search bar.
🔄 Persistent Across Sessions: Your pinned items and pages are automatically saved between sessions.
🔗 Scene Object Tracking with GUIDs: Ultimate Quick Inspector maintains its connection to GameObjects even if you rename or disable them.
